# The Broken Seal Society

![The Broken Seal Society](bss-seal.png)

---

## *Fides*

The seal is broken. The duty remains.

---

### About

The Broken Seal Society identifies practitioners who maintain the standard when systems do not.

No roster. No meetings. No explanations required.

---

### Recognition

Members receive a lapel pin: a broken wax seal bearing balanced scales and the word *Fides*.

You'll recognize the others.

---

### Membership

Selection is private. No applications accepted.

If you've received the pin, you know why.

---

### Contact

There is none.
